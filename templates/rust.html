Rust

What is Rust?

Rust is a fungal disease that affects many types of plants, forming reddish, brownish, or orange powdery pustules on leaves and stems. It weakens the plant by interfering with photosynthesis and reducing vigor and yield.

Symptoms

  • Small, rust-colored pustules on leaves and stems
  • Yellowing of surrounding tissue
  • Leaf distortion or curling
  • Premature leaf drop

Commonly Affected Plants

  • Wheat
  • Beans
  • Roses
  • Snapdragons

Causes

Caused by various species of the fungus *Puccinia* and *Uromyces*. Rust thrives in warm, moist environments and can spread via wind, rain, or infected plant material.

Prevention & Control

  • Grow resistant plant varieties
  • Remove and destroy infected leaves
  • Improve air circulation by proper pruning
  • Apply sulfur-based or systemic fungicides
