About PlantCare AI

PlantCare AI is an advanced Leaf Disease Detection System powered by a custom Convolutional Neural Network (CNN). Our mission is to provide farmers, botanists, and home gardeners with an accessible tool to instantly diagnose plant health issues and receive actionable remedies.

How It Works

Our system uses a custom NumPy-based CNN for feature extraction, identifying intricate patterns in leaf structures. It then utilizes Nearest-Neighbor Classification to compare the uploaded image features against a pre-trained dataset of healthy and diseased plant leaves.

Technologies Used

  • Backend: Python, Flask
  • AI/ML: Custom CNN implemented in NumPy
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Glassmorphism UI
