About PlantCare

PlantCare is an intelligent web-based system designed to help farmers, gardeners, and agricultural experts quickly identify plant diseases through image recognition.

Users simply upload a photo of a plant leaf, and the system uses a trained machine learning model to detect and classify diseases such as leaf spots, rusts, blights, and more.

Our goal is to support sustainable agriculture by offering accessible, fast, and reliable plant health diagnostics — even in remote locations where expert guidance is not immediately available.

This tool is built using modern technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript for the front-end, and Python, Flask, and Machine Learning for the backend. The model is trained on thousands of plant leaf images to ensure accurate results.

Together, let’s promote healthier crops, reduce chemical misuse, and empower plant lovers with technology. 🌱
